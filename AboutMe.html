About Me

I'm a Visual Effects gradute who studied at Escape Studios. I specialise in 2D compositing using industry level softawre, such as nuke and silhouette in a range of projects both as a part of my studies and freelance. I have experience in a wide range of task and tools including, compositing CG assets, Plate clean up, Rotoscoping and more.

Along side my VFX studies I try to take part in a wide range of hobbies to devlope skills I find interesting or enjoy doing. Some of these include Computer science and programming, athletics and motorsports, as well as art. I want to always be ready to continue learning and developing my skillset with any oppertunity I can find.
