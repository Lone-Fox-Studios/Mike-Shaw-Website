About Me

My passion for effects and the visual arts comes young from the inspiring effects of movies such as Tron or The Matrix. My love and fascination for thr worlds created on the screen lead me to pursue learning more about computer and technology as well as the arts. I therefore completed my A-level in computer science, art and design and mathematics.

I then pursued my passion further by attending Escape studios to study Visual Effects, which I believed would perfectly suit my love for both the computer technology and the story's of film. Here I built a foundational understanding for the entire post-production pipeline from taking part in modules from 3D Modeling, cinematography, animation and my specialism Composting. I was able to develop my skills in compositing further over the next two years where we studied many techniques from clean-up and tracking to greenscreen and matte painting.

In 2025, I graduated from Escape Studios with first class honors. Having completed the 3 year course I now had proficient knowledge in a wide variety of aspect covering the entire range of the post-production pipeline at an industry level.



Beyond Studies

I always want to make i'm pushing myself to the limit whether it is on the track with my body or on a computer creating something amazing.
